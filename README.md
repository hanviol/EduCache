**EduCache** (Offline Learning) is a powerful Flutter application designed to provide high-quality educational content to users in areas with limited or no internet connectivity. By caching and downloading courses from repositories like MIT OpenCourseWare and OpenLearn, EduCache ensures that learning never stops.

## ğŸš€ Features

- **Offline Access:** Download and study courses without an active internet connection.
- **Rich Content Support:** Includes video lectures, PDF documents, and interactive text-based lessons.
- **Educational Repositories:** Leverages content from MIT OCW, OpenLearn, and Internet Archive.
- **Progress Tracking:** Keep track of your learning journey with detailed lesson and course completion metrics.
- **Smart Search:** Find courses across various subjects including Computer Science, Mathematics, Science, and more.
- **Premium UI:** A modern, intuitive interface with smooth animations and dark mode support.
